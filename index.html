About Us

The Man Like Politics podcast brings political discussions to the everyday listener. Covering everything from the political influence of pop-culture to discussions on nuclear weapons, if it's got a political influence, we're here for it! Produced and hosted by Pouneh Ahari, Camarley McIntosh and Furnisha Lycett, this podcast is here to bring fresh discussions to the UK political scene.
